FEATURES

A brief exposition about the features we plan to provide

  • Traffic statistics
  • Accident prevention
  • Platforms and technologies

It's easier when you know what you'll find

One of our main focus is to deliver a service that makes quotidian life much easier, like facilitate the everyday trip to work or the sporadic walk to the beach. With that in mind, we tought on the following services:

  • Provide information about traffic
  • Detect affluence to a specific zone, allowing second thoughts related with the beach walk
  • Store and deliver quantitive information about inflow of vehicles or pedestrians in specific areas
  • Deliver ambiental information associated with vehicle pollution

It's not autonomous driving, yet :)

We live in an era that despite all the technology, road accidents continue to be one of the major causers of pain. Knowing that, we decided to do something about it and have the following planed functionalities:

  • Warn vehicle drivers that someone has intention to overtake them
  • Notify vehicle drivers about anomalous objects on the road, like trees or even immobilized vehicles
  • Provide information about climate conditions

5G On Command

5G is coming and with it comes new possibilities in telecomunications, like the future very famous VNF's. 5G isn't the only innovative aspect of our project, so in the next list we show a few more ...

  • Use of VNF to secure communications between sensors/cameras and the backend infrastructure
  • Implement a dynamic distributed system that permits better management of resources
  • Web Application, were the computed and stored data can be displayed
  • REST API granting acess to all the services provided by us
